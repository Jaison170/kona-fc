Frequently asked questions and support

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I become a member?

Visit our Membership page and choose a plan. Then contact us via the Contact page to complete your registration.

Where are home matches played?

All home matches are played at Kona Grounds, Nairobi. Check the Fixtures page for upcoming match dates.

How do I order from the shop?

Browse the Shop page and click Order Now on any item. You'll be directed to our Contact page to complete your order.

How can I contact the club?

Email us at info@konafc.co.ke or call +254 700 000 000. You can also use the Contact form on our Contact page.
