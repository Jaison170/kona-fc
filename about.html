Our Mission

Kona FC was founded in 2012 with one goal: to give Nairobi's youth a pathway into competitive football. What started as a small community side has grown into one of the city's most decorated clubs, with 23 trophies and four consecutive league titles. But silverware isn't the full story — we exist to inspire the next generation, building character, discipline, and pride both on and off the pitch.

Find Us

Kona Grounds is located off Thika Road, Nairobi. We welcome fans, players and partners to visit us during training sessions every Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday from 4pm.
